They called it *Breakfast Club*.

他们叫它“早餐俱乐部”。
但其实是夜的余波——

not morning, not yet release.

if you stayed long enough—

只要你熬过雾、最后一曲、

the ash pauses,

香烟间隙中的空白帧，

someone would emerge, like code from a hidden folder,

端出热豆浆，油条在纸袋里冒着热气，

a kind of offering.

我们蹲在楼梯上吃着，妆早已花了，

lipstick = smeared timestamp

eyes = echo chamber.

grinning like schoolkids from a field trip that never ended.

笑得像从不需要回家的中学生。

::celebration indexed beyond climax::

sustenance = communion

共享的卡路里 = 幸存的证据

dawn flickered at the edge of the script.

黎明像崩溃前的加载条。

temporary truce initiated.

我没敢关机。
