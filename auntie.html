She moved through the night like a rumor,
她在夜里穿行，像一个没人证实过的传闻—
not dancing, 不是在跳舞，
but sweeping time off the floor. 而是在地板上打扫时间的痕迹。

Her broom caught the echoes, 她的扫帚扫过回音，
and the glitter, 还有闪粉，
and the soft husks of names we no longer answered to.
还有那些我们早已不再回应的旧名字的壳。

打扫的阿姨整晚都在。
She stayed the whole night,
耳塞封住世界，earplugs sealing the world,
但她还是听见了一切。

The queens spun like planets. 皇后们像行星那样自转。
She paused. 她停下来看。
She didn’t flinch when the bass dropped. 低音坠落时，她纹丝不动。
She didn’t blink when someone sobbed into their sleeve.
有人在角落里哭，她没眨眼。

她望着舞台，像在看一场不断重写的神话。
She watched the stage like it was myth being rewritten in real time.

Someone offered her a drink. 有人递了一杯酒给她。
她笑了，像一个知道太多的人。
She laughed—soft, knowing.
她没接。 And didn’t take it.
